What is the inspiration behind DPL?

DPL is a modern parcel locker that combines a website and a parcel locker to improve the online shopping experience. DPL provides a safe and secure locker that courier companies can place customer packages inside. They enable online shoppers to pick up their packages without ever having to go to a postal office or be stuck at home waiting on their package to arrive. Additionally, DPL eliminates the threat of intruders stealing customer packages from their doorsteps.

We analyzed market trends for parcel storage lockers and briefly reviewed similar products in the market.
